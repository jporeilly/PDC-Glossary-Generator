Dear Mr. Wilson,

Great question. Arizona Water Company uses a conservation-oriented tiered rate
so that essential use stays affordable while discretionary use carries a higher
price. For a residential account in the Sedona system:

  Base charge:                 $35.50 / month
  Tier 1 (0-10,000 gal):       $3.50 per 1,000 gal
  Tier 2 (10,001-25,000 gal):  $5.25 per 1,000 gal
  Tier 3 (25,001-50,000 gal):  $7.50 per 1,000 gal
  Tier 4 (50,001+ gal):        $9.75 per 1,000 gal

At 14,000 gallons you'd pay the base charge, the full Tier 1 rate on the first
10,000 gallons, and the Tier 2 rate on the remaining 4,000 gallons. Shifting
about 4,000 gallons of outdoor watering to cooler hours could keep you closer
to Tier 1.

We're happy to schedule a free home water-use review. Just reply to this email.
